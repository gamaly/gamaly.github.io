Projects
PHR Management and Analytics

In 2018, a close family member was diagnosed with a rare blood disease called Severe Aplastic Anemia. To help us track routine Combined Blood Cell (CBC) tests and medications, I built a Personal Health Records (PHR) management system. The tool combines a simple to use data management back end with a series of charts and graphs. Most interestingly (I think), the system runs a set of linear regression models to check for correlations between variables and make predictions on white blood cell counts.

Site Code
Tools: R/Shiny, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Spa Creek Water Testing

Spa Creek Conservancy in Annapolis, MD conducts weekly water tests in over a dozen sites in Spa Creek and Back Creek. This platform provides a weekly update on results, keeps a running analysis of the correlation between total rainfall and test results, and shows the distribution of scores by testing site
